## Attendance Tracking App Proposal – Equestrian Classes

**Project Fee (MVP): ₹1,70,000**  
**Payment Schedule: 10 months, two installments per month**

---

### 1. Project Overview

This proposal covers the end‑to‑end design and development of an attendance tracking application for equestrian classes, including:

- Mobile app for iOS and Android (single cross‑platform codebase)  
- Web‑based admin dashboard  
- Backend and secure data storage  
- Manual payment tracking (with screenshot/manual entry)  
- Requirement gathering, UI/UX design, testing, deployment and handover  

The goal is to digitise class attendance and payment tracking so that admins and coaches have a simple, reliable tool tailored to their daily workflows.

---

### 2. Scope and Key Deliverables

#### 2.1 Mobile App (iOS & Android)

- Secure login and role‑based access (admin and coach focused for MVP)  
- Class schedule view for coaches (daily/weekly)  
- Rider list per class with basic details  
- Attendance marking: Present/Absent, with optional notes  
- Attendance history by rider or class with date filters  
- Read‑only view of payment status (Paid/Pending) for each rider  

#### 2.2 Admin Web Dashboard

- Rider and coach management (create, edit, deactivate)  
- Class and schedule management (create classes, assign coaches and timings)  
- Attendance overview and simple reports:  
  - Attendance by class, date range and rider  
  - Option to export basic reports (for example, CSV)  
- Manual payment tracking:  
  - Create and update payment records with amount, month/period, status (Pending/Paid) and notes  
  - Upload and view screenshots/photos as payment proof  
  - Filter by rider, month and payment status  

#### 2.3 Backend, Storage, QA and Launch

- Backend to store users, riders, classes, attendance and payment records  
- Storage for uploaded payment screenshots  
- Testing across representative Android and iOS devices  
- Support for app store submissions (Google Play and Apple App Store)  
- Deployment of backend and admin dashboard  
- Short admin usage guide and final handover  

#### 2.4 Discovery and Design

- Requirement workshops to understand current process and refine workflows  
- User flows and wireframes for key journeys  
- High‑fidelity UI designs for main mobile and admin screens  
- Up to two review cycles for design feedback
